ABOUT PROJECT FORGET ME

“Project Forget Me” is a covert scientific experiment designed to manipulate memory and control human subjects through forced amnesia and psychological conditioning. The project documents multiple test subjects and their deteriorating grasp on reality as the experiment progresses.

This platform serves as an archive for research data, secure notes, and logs from both current and past subjects, hidden from public view. Users authorized to access sensitive data must proceed with caution, as the consequences of exposure are unknown.
